Glossary

A glossary is a list of terms and their definitions, typically found at the end of a book, article, or document. Glossaries are often used to provide definitions of technical or specialized terms that may be unfamiliar to the reader.

What?

A Java program that outputs a group of HTML files given a text file listing terms and their definitions. There is a top-level index that lists each term in the glossary and separate pages for each of the terms with their definitions. Clicking on a term in the index shall take you to the page for that term and its associated definition. Moreover, clicking on any term in the glossary that happens to appear in a definition shall take you to the page for that term and its associated definition.

How?

Used Java to write and compile entire project. Utilized the OSU CSE components library for data types that read in, write, and sort data.

Results

Satisfied project requirements. Scored 11/10.
